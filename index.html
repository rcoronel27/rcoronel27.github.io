About this portfolio

This portfolio is to show the diversity of my writing and projects. The work I am displaying is what I am most proud of.

Artifacts

This is a research poster completed in December 2024, a culmination of my work in the First-Year Innovation & Research Experience (FIRE). I worked with a group of three others and a faculty advisor to analyze if the opening of the DC Metro Silver Line affected nearby housing prices.

This artifact is a critical analysis completed in March 2025 of 1970s Gourmet magazine where I compared its portrayal of Singapore as a colonial vacation spot against a modern food documentary that highlighted the Peranakan culture of the Singaporean people.

This artifact is a white paper I completed in May 2025. I took care to create a visually appealing yet research-based document to convince readers to attend a Talk Story event hosted by the 1882 Foundation.

This artifact is a Photoshop project completed in April 2025 where I emphasized the concept of an information detox we learned in class, and I drew inspiration from two other posters.

Personal Statement

As an information science major, I want to use my skills in data analysis to create good in the world. In fact, I switched my major from computer science to information science because I worried I wasn't developing the skills I wished. In addition, as an Asian American studies minor, I have a fervent interest in the preservation of Asian American culture and history, and I am a firm advocate for diversity in any team, workplace, or situation I find myself in.
